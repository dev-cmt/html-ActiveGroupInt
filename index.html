About AGI

Our Goal: Student

Access To Top Global Universities

AGI, established in 1999, is a dynamic multinational company with a strong presence in Australia, Bangladesh, and Japan, specializing in telecommunications, hospitality, healthcare, agriculture, and education & immigration consultancy. Leveraging extensive networks and relationships, AGI is poised to lead in the rapidly expanding education market in Bangladesh and the Sub-continent, offering unparalleled opportunities for student and business communities alike.

Our goal is to provide students with diverse academic opportunities by partnering with top universities worldwide through our extensive network.
